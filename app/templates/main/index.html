Spaghetti Carbonara

Pasta

A classic Italian pasta dish with bacon, eggs, and Parmesan cheese.

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Servings: 4

Steps:

  1. Boil water in a large pot and cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a skillet, cook bacon until crispy. Remove bacon and drain excess fat, leaving about 2 tablespoons in the skillet.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Add cooked spaghetti to the skillet with bacon fat, toss to coat, then remove from heat.
  5. Pour egg mixture over the spaghetti and quickly toss to combine. The heat from the pasta will cook the eggs and create a creamy sauce.
  6. Crumble cooked bacon and sprinkle over the top. Serve immediately.
